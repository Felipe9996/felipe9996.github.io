About me

Hi there! My name is Juan Felipe, also known as Felipe9999. I'm 17 years old, and aspire to become a software engineer in the future. I enjoy programming, videogames, and travelling around the world, especially to places that not many people know. I also like offroading, both as a way of entertaining myself and because it lets me go deep inside nature while still in the comfort of my car. I've started this blog as a way to share my trips around the world with the world, while also making the website from scratch for fun.
